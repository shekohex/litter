Litter is a native mobile client for Codex.

Connect to local or remote Codex servers, manage coding sessions from anywhere, and keep conversations moving without opening your laptop.

Features:
- Discover Codex servers on your local network automatically
- Connect to remote machines over SSH and start Codex remotely
- Talk to Codex with voice input for fast, hands-free prompts
- Review conversation history, reasoning, code blocks, and images in a mobile-native UI
- Stay connected to the servers you control instead of sending your work through a hosted relay

Litter is open source and built for people who want their coding agent with them wherever they are.
